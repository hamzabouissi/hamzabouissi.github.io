The sun rose, he grabbed his hoe on his back and started walking into the livelihood. A white conical hat, a black cotton shirt over loose cotton pants.
A set of clothes that hides his waiting soul, his way to the land used to be crowded, but now it's still crowded with thoughts that keep him busy.

He remembered his childhood when he used to lie with his father on a green field of rice hearing stories, but now neither his father nor the rice exists, he's lying alone on dry soil touching it and looking with sunk eyes for any sign of life.

His eyes filled with sadness, and his heart bumped from the fear of imagining how the future will be if he kept living in the same way. A dry soil, neighbors are leaving day after day because of drought and The village is nothing but a house of ghosts.

He thought of leaving with the neighbors, but the courage wasn't there for him, he wasn't ready to sacrifice his land, and his memories, he still has that little optimistic state of mind that he calls hope, a hope for a day that his land will flourish. 

Returning to home with a disturbed mind and clean clothes, he saw a man looking at his friend's house window, he approached him saying: "are you looking for something"

- Yeah, I was looking for the house owner, I'm a traveler, I get tired of walking, and it's nearly sundown, so I wanted to ask him if I can settle the night with him.
- this house is empty, its owner has left, but you can join me 
- yeah I appreciate your kindness, thanks
  
The traveler was sitting alone under a tree in front of the house, singing until he joins him
- the song, your face, they all seem sad.
- what you're seeing is civil war remnants. But I'm not seeing anything on you!
- anxiety left disturbance
- I was there before leaving my village, after the civil war ended, we were on the losing side, they took my source of living, and I couldn't afford to see my land taken without doing anything, I was disturbed between fighting until death or forgetting and moving and here I'm. Sometimes leaving is the logical way to a better life
- where are you heading now?
- to where life smiles for me again, I heard from some neighbors in my village, there is a village that has a fair ruler and if you become a good citizen they lend you land to cultivate, but they said the road isn't easy to bear. 
- I hope you replace your sadness and rage with a happy one.
- thanks, I got tired, am going to sleep , good night

He awaked the hidden feeling inside him...
