My journey began when &nbsp;I tried to create an open-source([Fissaa](https://github.com/hamzabouissi/fissaa)) tool that helps developers deploy their container on AWS (opponent to AWS copilot), It was a pleasant experience discovering the first time AWS Services.

Then I got recruited as DevOps Consultant with [pengguin](pengguin.com) 🐧where I was responsible for migrating the current stack using copilot from GCP -\> AWS &nbsp;and optimizing the resources for the lowest cost. The job at pengguin has opened my eye to many caveats and optimization hacks and to where AWS Services reaches its limit.

Now here I'm sharing my little experience with IT(DevOps, Python, C#) in general, I will be sharing my mistakes, my way of thinking, and the solution.
